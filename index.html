A Contemporary Carnatic Progressive Rock Band

We Are

Agam is a Bangalore-based contemporary Carnatic progressive rock band. The band was formed in the year 2003. An unique band that integrates Carnatic music with rock to bring new musical experience from both the worlds.

Checkout Popular Tracks

Here are some youtube videos of the popular tracks from AGAM

  • Swans of Saraswati

    Swans of Saraswati A.K.A Bantureethi Koluviyya Rama is one of the most popular Thyagaraja Krithis. The song was written in Hamsanaadam and Aadi Thalam.

  • Mist of Capricorn

    Manavyalakincharadate is another Saint Thyagaraja's Krithis written in raaga Nalinakaanti set to Deshadi Taala.

  • Rangapura Vihaara

    Rangapura Vihaara, a beautiful composition by Muthuswami Dikshitar on Sri Ranganatha Swamy. Written in the raaga Brindavana Saaranga set to Roopaka Taala.

  • Onwards and Upwards

    Subrahmanyena Rakshitoham, another composition by Muthuswami Dikshitar on Lord Subrahmanya Swamy. Written in the raaga Shuddha Dhanyasi set to Aadi Taala.

  • The Boat Song

    The boat song, a Malayalam contemporary Carnatic progressive rock number, is an original composition of band Agam.
